Hi, there! My name is Charles(Xiaochang) Li. I am currently a (2022-x)-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at William&Mary. I am fortunately advised by Prof. Huajie Shao. Prior to the Ph.D. journey, I received my M.S. degree of Data Science from East China Normal University fortunately advised by Prof. Xuesong Lu and Prof. Qiwen Dong, focusing on Natural Language Processing and Data Mining.

Currently, my research interest focuses on Foundation Model and Multi-Modal Reasoning. My goal is to empower multi-modal models with reasoning capabilities to solve complex real-world problems like human.

Recently, I have proposed a knowledge-guided foundation model for network traffic classification and generation tasks, which is still under review. Besides, I am actively working on multi-modal reasoning, any collaboration is welcome!

I am open to various forms of research collaboration. I am also open to any questions and discussions on ML, NLP and LLM. Feel free to leave any comments or send me an email! Looking forward to hear from you!

I enjoy watching good movies, and listening to music during my spare time. Besides, I like sports, including playing basketball, weight lifting, skiing and so on.
