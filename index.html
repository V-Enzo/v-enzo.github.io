Hi, there! My name is Charles(Xiaochang) Li. I am currently a (2022-x)-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at William&Mary. I am fortunately advised by Prof. Huajie Shao. Prior to the Ph.D. journey, I received my M.S. degree of Data Science from East China Normal University, focusing on Natural Language Processing and Data Mining.

My research interest focuses on Network Foundation Model and Large Language Model(LLM). My goal is to design practical Network Foundation Models to solve network traffic analysis problems.

Recently, we worked on both network traffic classification(understanding) tasks and generation tasks (Check our paper LENS) Besides, we have organized a network traffic benchmark and will be released soon.

!!! I am open to various forms of research collaboration. I am open to any questions and discussions on ML, NLP and LLM. Feel free to leave any comments or send me an email! Looking forward to hear from you!

I enjoy watching good movies, and listening to music during my spare time. Besides, I like sports, including playing basketball, weight lifting, skiing and so on.
